Vista Weather TV - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Vista Weather TV is a relaxing Android TV weather app that displays real-time weather conditions with ambient background music and dynamic visuals. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

Vista Weather TV does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app only retrieves weather data from public weather APIs and displays it locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party APIs such as AccuWeather or Supabase for weather data and background images. These services are used solely to provide app functionality and do not collect personal information from users.

Data Security

No personal or sensitive user data is transmitted to our servers. All weather information is processed on the device.
